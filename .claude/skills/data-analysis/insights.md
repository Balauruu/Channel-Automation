Accumulated learnings from skill runs. Append general insights only — no project-specific findings.

- Institutional Corruption is the most actionable gap signal: when no competitor has staked a primary claim in a pillar despite audience demand, that pillar should anchor the differentiation strategy, not just appear as one option among five
- Cluster quality with <100 videos collapses to single-word TF-IDF labels and many singleton clusters -- silhouette score optimization still selects max K, producing noise. Minimum viable corpus for meaningful clustering is ~200+ videos across 8+ channels; below that, keyword extraction is more useful than clustering.
