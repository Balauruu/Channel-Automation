- Wikimedia Commons image search using institution name variants (French and English) surfaces BAnQ-sourced historical photographs that are already public domain and high resolution -- search both "Saint-Jean-de-Dieu" and "Longue-Pointe" not just the English name.
- For Quebec documentary topics, CBC Archives and BAnQ together cover nearly all 1990s-2004 news footage needs -- CBC for English broadcast, BAnQ for institutional and government photographs; both require rights verification before use.
