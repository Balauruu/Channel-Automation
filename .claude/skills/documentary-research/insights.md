- [2026-04-11] For institutional abuse scandals involving government and church co-perpetrators, the financial motive (subsidy disparity, per-diem rates) is always the strongest opening hook -- it reduces abstract horror to a single arithmetic transaction that any viewer can follow.
- [2026-04-16] For well-documented historical topics, Wikipedia's wikitext (fetched via the MediaWiki API) functions as a pre-assembled source manifest: embedded citations include author, outlet, date, and often page numbers -- retrieving wikitext first dramatically reduces survey time compared to running separate searches.
