## Decisions
- [2026-04-10] Source hierarchy: court documents > government reports > contemporary news > books > retrospective articles > blogs > forums
- [2026-04-10] Every factual claim must trace to a cited source -- unsourced claims flagged as blockers, not advisories
- [2026-04-10] Research quality gate: minimum 3 primary source domains required before script generation proceeds
- [2026-04-10] Conflicting sources presented side-by-side with assessment -- never silently resolve by picking one
- [2026-04-10] Script word count target: 3,000-7,000 words (20-50 min at 150 words/min); act count: 4-7

## Patterns
- [2026-04-10] Wikipedia-only sourcing indicates insufficient research depth -- always verify downstream references in Wikipedia citations
- [2026-04-10] Timeline inconsistencies between sources should be presented side-by-side, not silently resolved by the researcher
- [2026-04-10] Modal qualifiers ("reportedly", "allegedly") on sourced factual claims are voice violations -- facts are stated as facts when sourced
- [2026-04-10] Vague open questions ("more research needed") indicate the researcher did not fully analyze what is missing -- push for specific questions with search strategies
