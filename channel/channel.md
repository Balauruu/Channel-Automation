# Channel DNA

## Identity

- **Genre:** Documentary
- **Niche:** Dark History, True Crime & Unsolved Mysteries
- **Platform:** Youtube
- **Format:** Deep dives into obscure topics (target: 20-50 min)
- **Style:** Cinematic, narrative-driven, clinically neutral, deadpan
- **Tone:** Calm, journalistic, deadpan -- let facts deliver the horror. No cheap sensationalism.
- **Voice:** Third-person narrator. The gravity comes from the story, not performance.
- **What works:** The neutrality creates unease. The depth creates trust. Audience feels like they're discovering something hidden.

---

## Core Content Pillars

1. **Obscure Historical Crimes** -- Real events buried by time. Unknown to mainstream audiences. High shock factor when revealed.
2. **Cults & Psychological Control** -- Groups, ideologies, or figures that bent reality for followers. Strong visual and narrative potential.
3. **Unsolved Disappearances & Mysteries** -- Cases with no clean resolution. Ambiguity is a feature, not a bug.
4. **Institutional Corruption & Cover-ups** -- Governments, corporations, and investigations that failed deliberately.
5. **Dark Web & Internet Underbelly** -- Digital crimes, underground communities, and online phenomena with real-world consequences.


---

## Topic Selection Criteria

A topic passes if it meets **at least 3 of 4**:

- [ ] **Obscurity** -- Not covered to death by mainstream media or large channels
- [ ] **Complexity** -- Multi-layered; reveals more the deeper you go
- [ ] **Shock Factor** -- A detail, revelation, or twist that genuinely disturbs
- [ ] **Verifiable** -- Based on documented sources, records, or credible reporting

**Hard Rejects:**
- Topics covered by our channel (see `past_topics.md`)

## Differentiation Strategy

| Axis | Competitors | Our Channel |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| Length | 10-90 min variable | 20-50 min target sweet spot |
| Topics | Internet mysteries + crime | Dark history + cults + crime + disappearances |
| Depth | Deep (Knudsen) / Investigative (BS) | Research-first, source-anchored |
| Angle | Varied | Focus on stories most people have never heard of |

**Our edge:** We combine Knudsen's research depth and neutral delivery with Barely Sociable's narrative pull and topic range -- but anchor everything in **obscure, documented history** rather than internet culture.

---

## Audience Profile

- **Age:** 22-38
- **Psychographic:** Intellectually curious, skeptical of mainstream narratives, drawn to dark and taboo subjects. Binge-watches documentary content. Values being informed over being entertained (though entertainment is the hook).
- **Platform behavior:** Watches long-form if the first 90 seconds justify the runtime.
